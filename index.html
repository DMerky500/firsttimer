Select your doctor by the 15th of the month prior to your plan's start date.
Don't forget to select your primary care doctor.

Call us at 855.494.1319 to choose your PersonalCare Partner System and your primary care doctor. You will receive your ID card after you select your partner system and your doctor.

If we don’t receive your partner system and doctor choice by the 15th of the month prior to your plan’s start date, we will select on your behalf based on where you live, and will then send you your ID card.

You can select from one of five partner systems:
  1. EvergreenHealth Partners
  2. MultiCare Connected Care
  3. Northwest Physicians Network
  4. UW Medicine Accountable Care Network
  5. Virginia Mason Medical Center
When choosing your partner system, consider whether you already have a doctor in one of the systems and which system offers care locations close to your home or work.

You must choose a doctor within your partner system. Non-emergency services are only covered outside your chosen partner system when you are referred by your primary care doctor. In most cases, if you get care from a provider not in your partner system, you pay the full cost.
